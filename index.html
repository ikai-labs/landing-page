Generation of slides

Example: Creating a "Why IKAI" section

1

RFP Assessment & Strategic Positioning

IKAI assesses the RFP requirements to develop a compelling section on why this company should be selected. The AI analyzes evaluation criteria, client needs, and competitive positioning to craft a strategic "why choose us" narrative grounded in your firm's unique strengths.

2

Template Selection, Content Sourcing and storytelling

IKAI considers available templates and previous presentations to be leveraged. The AI identifies relevant slide formats from your firm's library and extracts reusable content from past proposals, and generates new slides ensuring brand consistency and building on proven messaging.

3

First Version Generated

IKAI creates the first version by combining the selected template with the RFP requirements. The AI generates a complete slide with strategic messaging, on-brand formatting, and content tailored to address the client's specific evaluation criteria.

4

Iterate & Refine with AI Assistant

Interact with IKAI's assistant to iterate on the slide content. Through conversational refinement, you can adjust messaging, strengthen key points, or tailor the narrative—all while maintaining your template's structure and brand standards.

5

Updated Version Delivered

Receive the updated version of your slide incorporating all refinements from the iteration. The improved content reflects your feedback while preserving the professional formatting and brand alignment—ready for final review or further refinement.
